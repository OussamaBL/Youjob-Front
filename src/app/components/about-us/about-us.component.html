Our Story

Founded in 2020, YouJob started with a simple idea: make it easier for people to find reliable, skilled professionals for their home improvement and repair needs.

We recognized the challenges both customers and service providers face in connecting with each other. Customers struggle to find trustworthy professionals, while skilled tradespeople have difficulty marketing their services effectively.

Our platform bridges this gap by creating a marketplace where customers can easily find and hire qualified professionals, and where skilled tradespeople can showcase their expertise and grow their business.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Journey

How we've grown and evolved over the years

2020

Company Founded

YouJob was founded with the mission to connect skilled professionals with customers in need of their services.

2021

Platform Launch

We launched our platform with 500 registered professionals and expanded to serve 5 major cities.

Rapid Growth

Our user base grew to over 10,000 customers and 1,000 service providers. We expanded our service categories and improved our platform.
